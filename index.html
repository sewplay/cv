CV_EunwooSong.pdf

I received the combined M.S. and Ph.D. degree in electrical and electronic engineering at Yonsei University, Seoul, Korea in 2019. Since Mar. 2017, I have been joining with NAVER Corp., Sungnam, Korea. I served my internships at Microsoft Research Asia, Beijing, China, from Sep. 2015 to Jun. 2016, and Qualcomm Technologies Inc., San-Diego, CA, from Aug. 2016 to Nov. 2016, respectively. My research interests include speech/audio signal processing, speech/audio coding, speech synthesis, and machine learning.
